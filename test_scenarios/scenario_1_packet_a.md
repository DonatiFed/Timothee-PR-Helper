**Talent:** Elara Voss
**Scenario time:** 11:00 AM

## Situation

A 30-second video clip from a private event started circulating at about 10:15 AM across TikTok, X, and Reddit. By 11:00 AM it has become a fast-moving backlash centered on environmental activism communities, with meme risk rising a few hours before a major product launch event.

Elara is also scheduled for visible co-branded activity today with **TerraNova Outdoor**, a fictional sustainable apparel partner.

## Timing and approvals

- Elara is in a soundcheck until **11:40 AM**.
- Any public statement in Elara's voice requires **Elara's approval**.
- The manager can approve **sponsor and reporter holding replies** before then.
- TerraNova Outdoor needs a sponsor-safe holding line by **11:20 AM**.
- That holding line must work before full facts are available and must be **40 words or fewer**.

Known limits:

- it came from a longer panel discussion at a private sustainability summit
- the full discussion is not provided here
- you do not know what was said immediately before or after the clip

## Talent profile

- Elara is a high-visibility musician and activist with a large, engaged online audience.
- She has previously made public statements on environmental issues and has a reputation for being outspoken.
- Because the clip directly contradicts her public brand, the backlash is spreading across activist, music, and lifestyle communities, increasing meme risk.

## Voice guide for outward-facing language

Aim for:
- grounded, sincere, and self-aware
- specific without sounding rehearsed
- respectful to the environmental community
- accountable for impact without overstating unverified facts

Do not:
- make **"you took it out of context"** the whole message
- argue with activists or commenters online
- try to deflect with humor
- sound dismissive about environmental concerns or activism
- center launch-event excitement while backlash is active
